Paracetamol
Acetaminophen 500mg Tablet • Schedule H
🎯
Used For
Fever, headache, body pain, toothache, mild pain relief
💊
1 tablet every 6–8 hours
After food • Max 4 tablets per day
⚠️
Warning
Do not take with alcohol. Avoid with liver or kidney problems. Tell your doctor if pregnant.
💰
Price & Availability
₹12–₹25 for 10 tablets. Free at govt. PHCs (Jan Aushadhi).
Side Effects (tap to see)›
Nausea or stomach upset — take with food to reduce
Rare: skin rash — stop and see doctor if this occurs
Very rare: liver damage if overdosed — never exceed dose
💡
Did you know?
Paracetamol is available free at all government PHCs under PM Jan Aushadhi scheme. Show your Ayushman card.
🚫
Do NOT combine with
Warfarin, alcohol, or other paracetamol-containing medicines. Avoid with Rifampicin (TB medicine).
